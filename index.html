About Me

My name is Eric Morgan and this is my second semester here at OTC. I'm originally from Cape Girardeau,MO, where I grew up in the bootheel. Cape Girardeau is located right on top of the Mississippi River and would flood pretty frequently in spring and sometimes summer. It was a nice little place to live, but it's very small compared to Springfield and you're more likely to get attacked by mosquitoes.

Before I enrolled in college here, I attended high school at Kickapoo where I marched in the Golden Arrow Band for all four years. We competed at places like Ozark, Broken Arrow, Tulsa, and finally St. Louis at the end of the season. At St. Louis, we competed at a massive stadium called the dome. Though it was fun to compete and hang out with my friends, but my interest for computer science took over, which got me here.
