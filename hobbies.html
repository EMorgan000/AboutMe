Hobbies

From an early age, I was, and still am, a bit of an outdoorsman. Every now and then I go fishing with Dad, and help him with his horse at a friends' farm. Otherwise, I like to game and draw in my free time. I stick to playing single-player games due to not having as much time on my hands as before. I like many different genres of games and I'm willing to try just about any kind.

When I'm drawing, I usually draw portraits from references I find using Pinterest. Lately, I've been getting into figure drawing to get out of my comfort zone. Art also gives me another way to unwind after doing schoolwork.
